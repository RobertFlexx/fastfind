# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be open, direct, and respectful.

Contributors, maintainers, and users should feel safe participating regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

* Be respectful and constructive.
* Assume good intent, especially in technical disagreements.
* Give actionable feedback focused on code and behavior.
* Be clear and specific when reporting issues.

## Unacceptable behavior

* Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
* Trolling, insults, or intentionally disruptive behavior
* Publishing private information without consent
* Repeated bad-faith arguments after moderator guidance

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation for behavior that violates this code.

For serious concerns, open a private report through the repository security/contact channel.
